Dr. Frouws focuses on symptom-driven hormone care with a thorough assessment process, careful lab review, and individualized planning. Visits are structured around clarity — understanding what’s happening, identifying contributing factors, and building a plan you can realistically follow.

Care often includes education, lifestyle and nutrition foundations, and evidence-informed medical options where appropriate — with follow-up and adjustments based on how you respond.
