About

What this service does

Results you may expect

  • Fast hydration support (fluids + electrolytes)
  • May support recovery after travel, illness, or a busy week
  • Wellness-focused blends for energy, immune, and antioxidant support
  • Options can be customized with add-ons as appropriate

Ideal for

  • Dehydration, fatigue
  • Recovery from illness, travel, intense workouts
  • Fitness recovery support
  • Vitamin deficiency correction
  • Liver and detoxification support

How it works

A customized blend of vitamins and minerals is mixed in an IV bag and delivered directly into the bloodstream. Because it bypasses the digestive system, it can offer higher absorption than oral supplements. At your visit, a doctor will review your history, do a quick physical exam, and determine your suitability for IV therapy. If appropriate, your doctor will then customize an IV drip tailored to your needs. Your infusion is delivered over a controlled timeframe, and your comfort is monitored throughout.

Service details

Time

Most IV treatments take 30–75 minutes, depending on the type of infusion and the vitamins included.

Downtime

None. Most patients can return to normal activities right away unless their provider advises otherwise.

Customization

Your IV can be tailored to your goals, with wellness-focused blends and add-ons used where appropriate.

Safety screening

Before treatment, we review your health history to make sure the infusion is safe and appropriate for you.

Comfort

Most people feel only a small pinch when the IV is inserted, then relax comfortably while the infusion runs.

Before your visit

We recommend eating a light meal and drinking water before your appointment to help you feel more comfortable during the infusion.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Who should avoid IV therapy? +
IV therapy may not be recommended for people with certain medical conditions, including:
  • Kidney disease
  • Heart failure
  • Severe allergies to any of the vitamins used
  • Certain chronic health conditions
Our providers will review your history before treatment and determine if IV therapy is indicated and safe for you.
What does IV vitamin therapy help with? +
IV therapy may help support:
  • Hydration
  • Energy levels
  • Recovery from illness or travel
  • General wellness
Please note that IV vitamin therapy is currently under studied and is not a proven treatment.
Can I eat before my IV? +
Yes. We recommend eating a light meal and drinking water before your appointment to help you feel more comfortable during the infusion.
Are there any side effects? +
Side effects are uncommon but may include:
  • Mild bruising at the IV site
  • Feeling cold during the drip
  • Temporary lightheadedness
Serious reactions are rare, and you are monitored during the treatment.
Is IV therapy covered by MSP or insurance? +
IV vitamin therapy is considered a private service and is not covered by MSP. Some extended health plans may cover treatments provided by a naturopath or physician. We offer direct billing to most insurance companies.
How do I book an appointment? +
Choose the IV therapy option you would like and click the Book Now button. You will be redirected to our secure booking system to select a time.
