About

What this service does

Results you may expect

  • Fast hydration support (fluids + electrolytes)
  • May help you feel more “recovered” after travel, illness, or a busy week
  • Wellness-focused blends for energy, immune support, and antioxidant support
  • Options can be customized with add-ons (where appropriate)

Ideal for

  • Dehydration, low fluid intake, or post-travel fatigue
  • Busy weeks when you’re run down
  • Fitness recovery and “reset” days
  • Clients who prefer medical oversight and sterile delivery

How it works

A trained clinician reviews your history, checks vitals, and starts a sterile IV line. Your infusion is delivered over a controlled timeframe. We monitor comfort throughout.

FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does an IV drip take? +
Most drips run 30–60 minutes depending on the blend, your veins, and your comfort.
Is IV therapy safe? +
It can be safe when medically screened and delivered by trained clinicians using sterile technique. Some conditions and medications may make IV therapy inappropriate — we’ll screen before starting.
Who should avoid IV therapy? +
We may postpone or decline treatment for pregnancy/breastfeeding, kidney disease, certain heart conditions, uncontrolled blood pressure, active infections at the IV site, or specific allergies. Your provider will confirm.
What does it feel like? +
A quick pinch to place the IV, then most people feel normal while relaxing. Some nutrients can feel “warm” or cause a taste sensation — we can slow the drip if needed.
When will I notice results? +
Many notice hydration and a “reset” feeling the same day. For wellness-focused nutrient drips, responses vary by person and by underlying needs.
