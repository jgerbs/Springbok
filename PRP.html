Regenerative Therapy

PRP

PRP therapy uses the healing power of your own blood to help rejuvenate and restore. A small sample is drawn and processed to concentrate platelets, then applied or injected to support natural regeneration. PRP may be used for hair restoration or facial rejuvenation to support collagen production, skin quality, and overall revitalization.

About

What PRP treatment does

Results you can expect

  • Supports natural regeneration + healing
  • Promotes collagen production for improved texture
  • Helps hair look fuller and healthier over time
  • Boosts radiance and overall skin quality (facial rejuvenation)

Ideal for

  • Hair thinning / hair restoration support
  • Dullness + uneven skin texture
  • Clients wanting regenerative, natural-looking results

How it works

We draw a blood sample, spin it to concentrate the platelet rich plasma. This PRP is then applied or injected into the skin or scalp to stimulate natural repair, support collagen production, and promote healthier skin and hair growth over time. PRP can be paired with DP4 microneedling to enhance absorption and results.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Is PRP used for hair and face? +
Yes. PRP is commonly used for both hair restoration and skin rejuvenation. It can support hair growth in thinning areas and improve skin texture, tone, and collagen when used on the face, often combined with DP4 microneedling.
How many sessions will I need? +
Most patients benefit from a series of treatments. A common plan is three sessions spaced 1–3 months apart, followed by maintenance treatments if needed.
Is there downtime? +
Downtime is usually minimal. You may experience redness, swelling, or mild soreness for a few days depending on the area treated. Most people return to normal activities within 24–48 hours.
When will I see results? +
PRP works by stimulating your body’s natural healing response, so results develop gradually. Some improvement may be noticed within a few weeks, with continued changes over the following months.
