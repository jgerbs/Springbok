Regenerative Therapy

PRP

PRP therapy uses the healing power of your own blood to help rejuvenate and restore. A small sample is drawn and processed to concentrate platelets, then applied or injected to support natural regeneration. PRP may be used for hair restoration or facial rejuvenation to support collagen production, skin quality, and overall revitalization.

About

What PRP treatment does

Results you can expect

  • Supports natural regeneration and healing
  • Promotes collagen production for improved texture
  • Helps hair look fuller and healthier over time
  • Boosts radiance and overall skin quality

Ideal for

  • Hair thinning or hair restoration support
  • Dullness and uneven texture
  • Fine lines and early aging support
  • Clients wanting regenerative, natural-looking results

How it works

We draw a small blood sample, process it to concentrate platelets, then apply or inject PRP where needed. Platelet growth factors help signal repair, regeneration, and collagen remodeling.

Details

PRP service details

Time

75 minutes. Includes blood draw, processing, and treatment.

Format

In-clinic appointment. Treatment planning is tailored to your goals, whether for hair or facial rejuvenation.

Comfort

Comfort measures may be used. Sensation varies by treatment area and whether PRP is applied or injected.

Downtime

Varies. Mild redness, tenderness, or swelling may occur depending on the area treated.

Best results

A series is often recommended. Your provider will guide spacing based on your goals and response.

Aftercare

Follow aftercare instructions closely to support healing and the best possible results.
