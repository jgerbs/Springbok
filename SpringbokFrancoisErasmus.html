Dr. Erasmus provides evidence-based family medicine with an emphasis on prevention, long-term health planning, and continuity of care. Appointments focus on understanding what’s happening, outlining realistic options, and agreeing on a plan that’s clear and achievable.

When appropriate, Dr. Erasmus also offers select medical aesthetics services through Springbok Medical Spa — with consult-first planning and clear guidance on expectations, timing, and aftercare.

Treatment focus

  • Preventative care and long-term health planning
  • Evidence-based diagnostics and referrals
  • Chronic condition monitoring (for established patients)
  • Clear next steps with appropriate follow-up
