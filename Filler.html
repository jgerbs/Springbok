About

What this service does

Results you can expect

  • Restored midface or cheek support (lifted appearance)
  • Enhanced lip shape and hydration look (not “overdone”)
  • Softer appearance of folds (nasolabial / marionette lines)
  • More balanced proportions (chin / jawline definition)

Ideal for

  • Volume loss in cheeks or temples
  • Desire for more lip definition or symmetry
  • Structural contour goals (chin / jawline)
  • Clients wanting a “refreshed” look without surgery

How it works

We assess your facial structure and movement, then choose a filler type and placement plan. Treatment is built around balance: where to support, where to soften, and how to keep results looking like you.

Not sure if you need filler, Botox, or a skin treatment? Book a consult — we’ll guide you to the safest and most effective option.

FAQ

Questions, answered

How long do fillers last? +
Longevity varies by product, placement, and your metabolism. Some areas last longer than others. We’ll set realistic expectations at your consult.
Will I be swollen or bruised? +
Mild swelling and tenderness are common, especially for lips. Bruising can happen and varies person to person. Most clients feel presentable quickly, with refinement as swelling settles.
How much filler do I need? +
It depends on your anatomy and goals. We’ll recommend a conservative starting plan and you can always build gradually.
Is filler the same as Botox? +
They do different things: Botox relaxes muscles; filler adds support/volume. Many clients combine both for balanced results.
Who should avoid filler? +
We’ll review medical history, allergies, and any active skin infections. Certain situations (including pregnancy/breastfeeding) may mean postponing or choosing a different option.
