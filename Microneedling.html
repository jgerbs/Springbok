FAQ

Questions, answered

How many microneedling sessions do I need? +
It depends on your skin goals. Some clients start with one session, while others benefit from a series for texture, scars, or fine lines.
Is there downtime? +
Expect some redness and sensitivity after treatment, usually for 1 to 3 days depending on your skin and treatment depth.
What does SkinPen help improve? +
SkinPen can help improve the appearance of texture, tone, firmness, fine lines, enlarged pores, and certain types of scarring.
Is microneedling safe? +
When performed in a medical setting with proper screening and sterile technique, microneedling can be a safe, effective treatment for many clients.
Who should avoid microneedling? +
Active skin infections, certain inflammatory skin flare-ups, or other medical considerations may mean delaying treatment. Your provider will review safety with you first.
