About

What this service does

Plasma Pen (Plasma IQ) is a non-surgical treatment that uses plasma energy to create controlled micro-injuries on the skin’s surface — triggering collagen and elastin production for tightening and texture improvement.

Results you can expect

  • Visible tightening + lift in treated areas
  • Smoother texture + softened fine lines
  • Improved appearance of wrinkles + crepey skin
  • Collagen remodeling that continues over time

Ideal for

  • Fine lines + wrinkles
  • Mild-to-moderate skin laxity
  • Targeted areas needing lift + firming
  • Clients wanting a non-surgical option

How it works

Plasma energy creates a controlled treatment pattern that stimulates the skin’s natural repair response. Over time, collagen + elastin production helps tighten and refine the treated area.
