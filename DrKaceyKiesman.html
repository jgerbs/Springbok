Dr. Kacey Kiesman

Whole-person care with practical, sustainable next steps — blending root-cause thinking with modern clinical standards.

Focus Naturopathic medicine • Wellness planning
New clients Accepting

Born and raised in the small coastal town of Prince Rupert, Dr. Kiesman has always valued genuine connection and thoughtful care. She completed her nursing education in rural Northern BC, where she gained extensive hands-on clinical experience, early responsibility, and a strong foundation in safe, practical care.

Throughout her naturopathic medical program, she worked for four years as a Registered Nurse in a naturopathic clinic, delivering IV therapies and injections. This experience allowed her to blend clinical precision with a strong focus on preventative health and long-term wellbeing.

She has always been passionate about preventative health, whole-body wellbeing, and medical aesthetics. Blending health optimization with thoughtful, natural-looking aesthetic care has long been her goal, and she is grateful to now be working in a space that aligns closely with her values.

She looks forward to providing care that is experienced, safe, effective, and always tailored to each individual so patients can feel confident, supported, and at ease every step of the way.

Treatment focus

  • Root-cause assessment + step-by-step wellness plans
  • Stress, sleep, and energy support strategies
  • Nutrition + lifestyle guidance (realistic + sustainable)
  • Preventative care and long-term health maintenance
