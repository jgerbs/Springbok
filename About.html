Our Mission

Founded to create a better kind of care.

Why Springbok was founded

Springbok was founded out of a desire to create a more connected kind of care. Too often, patients are left moving between providers, repeating the same story, and trying to make sense of care that feels rushed or fragmented. The vision was to build a clinic where people could access thoughtful, well-rounded support in one place, with a team that works together rather than in isolation.

A big part of that vision was bringing together a broader range of expertise under one roof. With medical doctors, a naturopath, hormone-focused care, wellness services, and aesthetic treatments, Springbok was designed to offer patients more complete support without always sending them elsewhere for the next piece of the puzzle. The goal was not simply to offer more services, but to make care feel more cohesive and easier to navigate.

Springbok was also built on the belief that patients deserve to understand their care. People should not feel like they have to blindly trust recommendations without context or clarity. Taking time to explain options, answer questions, and make sure patients feel informed is an important part of how care is approached here, because better understanding leads to better decisions and greater confidence moving forward.

At its core, Springbok was founded because we genuinely care about helping people. The goal has always been to create a clinic that feels professional and modern, while still being personal, respectful, and grounded in real relationships. More than anything, Springbok was built to be a place where patients feel supported not just medically, but as people.

A broader care team

More perspectives on your care.

Springbok brings together experienced family physicians, a naturopath, and hormone-focused care within a single clinic. This integrated structure allows patients to benefit from a broader clinical perspective without the need to coordinate care across multiple locations.

Collaborative care

Care that works together.

Providers at Springbok work within a collaborative environment, consulting one another when appropriate to support well-informed clinical decisions. This approach helps ensure care plans are thoughtful, coordinated, and aligned with each patient’s needs.

03

Clear, informed care

You understand your care.

Patients are guided through their options with clear explanations and open discussion. The focus is on ensuring you understand your care, so decisions feel informed, appropriate, and aligned with your goals.

04

Clinic + wellness

More than just primary care.

In addition to family medicine and hormone care, Springbok offers a range of wellness and aesthetic services. This allows patients to access both medical and supportive treatments within a single, coordinated clinical environment.

05

Connected care network

Support beyond the clinic.

Through established relationships with trusted partners, including specialists such as plastic surgeons and concussion clinics, patients can be guided toward appropriate additional care in a more streamlined and supported manner when needed.

Springbok was built on a commitment to patient-focused care. This approach emphasizes respect, continuity, and meaningful relationships, creating an experience that feels supportive, attentive, and grounded in genuine care.
