About

What this service does

Results you can expect

  • Long-lasting hair reduction
  • Smoother skin with reduced shaving frequency
  • Targets multiple areas from face to full body
  • Comfort-forward treatment with integrated cooling

Ideal for

  • Unwanted facial or body hair
  • Ingrown hairs or irritation from shaving (where appropriate)
  • Clients wanting lower-maintenance grooming
  • Those ready for a consistent treatment plan

How it works

Diolaze laser hair removal uses targeted light energy to safely and effectively reduce unwanted hair. The device emits gentle laser pulses that are absorbed by the pigment in the hair. This energy travels down the hair shaft to the follicle, where it heats and disables the root to help prevent future growth.

Because hair grows in cycles, multiple treatments are recommended to target follicles during their active growth phase for optimal, long-lasting results. Diolaze features built-in cooling technology to enhance comfort, making treatment fast, precise, and suitable for a variety of skin types and treatment areas.

Details

Service details

Time

Appointments typically range from 30-90 minutes depending on the treatment area and whether you are booking a single area or a larger bundled service.

Customization

Treatment is tailored to your skin type, hair type, and the area being treated. Your provider will help select the most appropriate plan based on your goals.

Comfort

DiolazeXL is designed with built-in cooling to support comfort throughout the treatment. Most patients describe the sensation as quick snaps or warmth on the skin.

Safety screening

A consultation is recommended before treatment to review your history, skin and hair type, recent sun exposure, medications, and any factors that may affect safety or results.

Best results

Laser hair removal works best as a series because hair grows in cycles. Most patients need 6–10 sessions spaced several weeks apart for the most noticeable long-term reduction.

Aftercare

Downtime is minimal. Mild redness or sensitivity may occur for a few hours after treatment. Arrive with the area shaved, avoid waxing or plucking, limit sun exposure, and follow your provider’s aftercare guidance between visits.

FAQ

Questions, answered

When will I see results? +
Hair gradually becomes thinner and grows back more slowly and patchy after each session.

Noticeable reduction usually occurs after a few treatments, with continued improvement over the full series.
Can I wear makeup after treatment? +
Yes, makeup can usually be worn the same day if the skin is not irritated.

If there is redness or sensitivity, it may be best to wait until the skin returns to normal.
Who should avoid this treatment? +
Laser hair removal may not be recommended for people with:
  • Active skin infections or open wounds
  • Certain skin conditions in the treatment area
  • Recent sunburn or heavy tanning
  • Use of medications that increase light sensitivity
Your provider will review your history before treatment.
What areas can be treated with DiolazeXL? +
DiolazeXL can be used on most areas of the body, including:
  • Face
  • Underarms
  • Bikini area
  • Legs
  • Back
  • Chest
  • Arms
Your provider will help choose the best treatment plan based on your goals.
Is DiolazeXL permanent? +
Laser hair removal provides long-term hair reduction.

Some hair may grow back over time, but it is usually finer and lighter. Maintenance treatments may be recommended.
Does laser hair removal hurt? +
Most patients describe the feeling as a quick snap or warmth on the skin.

DiolazeXL is designed with built-in cooling to help keep the treatment as comfortable as possible.
Why do I need multiple sessions? +
Hair grows in cycles, and laser treatment only works on hairs in the active growth phase.

Multiple sessions are needed to treat all hairs effectively.
Do I need a consultation first? +
A consultation is recommended to review your skin and hair type, discuss your goals, and ensure the treatment is safe for you.
