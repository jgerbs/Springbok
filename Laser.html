About

What this service does

Results you can expect

  • Long-lasting hair reduction (series recommended)
  • Smoother skin with reduced shaving frequency
  • Targets multiple areas from face to full body
  • Comfort-forward treatment with integrated cooling

Ideal for

  • Unwanted facial or body hair
  • Ingrown hairs / irritation from shaving (where appropriate)
  • Clients wanting lower-maintenance grooming
  • Those ready for a consistent treatment plan

How it works

Laser energy is absorbed by pigment in the hair shaft and delivered to the follicle. Treatments are spaced to match hair growth cycles for best outcomes.

Not sure if you’re eligible or which area to book? Please book a Discovery Visit — we’ll guide you to the safest option for your skin + hair type.

Details

Service details

Time

Most appointments are 30–45 minutes (some bundles are longer).

Format

In-clinic laser hair removal. Settings are tailored to your skin + hair type.

Comfort

Cooling is built-in. Expect quick “snaps” of warmth during pulses.

Downtime

Typically none. Mild redness can occur and usually settles quickly.

Best results

A series of treatments is recommended (timing depends on area + growth cycle).

Prep / Aftercare

Arrive with the area shaved (not waxed). Avoid sun exposure and follow your provider’s guidance.

FAQ

Questions, answered

How many sessions do I need? +
Most people need a treatment series because hair grows in cycles. Your provider will tailor timing based on the area treated, hair type, and your goals.
Is there downtime? +
Usually very little. Mild redness or warmth can happen after treatment and often settles quickly.
When will I see results? +
Hair reduction happens gradually across a treatment series. Many clients notice slower regrowth and less dense hair after early sessions.
Do I need to shave before treatment? +
Yes. Most clients should arrive with the treatment area freshly shaved. Avoid waxing or plucking before treatment.
Who should avoid this treatment? +
We’ll review safety before treatment. Recent tanning, certain medications, active skin issues, or other medical considerations may mean delaying treatment or choosing another option.
