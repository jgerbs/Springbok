About

What this service does

Results you can expect

  • Softer-looking frown lines (11s), forehead lines, and crow’s feet
  • A more rested, youthful look
  • Lower face slimming in appropriate treatment areas
  • Preventative support for deepening expression lines over time
  • Therapeutic support for concerns such as migraines or hyperhidrosis when appropriate

Ideal for

  • Fine lines and expression lines
  • Clients wanting a low-downtime, natural refresh
  • Facial slimming goals
  • Jaw tension, clenching, or excess sweating in select areas

How it works

We assess your natural expressions, choose strategic injection points, and use conservative dosing to keep results soft and balanced. A two-week follow-up is scheduled to review your result, and small adjustments can be made if needed.

Details

Service details

Time

Appointments are typically 45–60 minutes, including consultation, treatment planning, and injections.

Best for

Fine lines, expression lines, subtle facial slimming, and clients wanting a refreshed look with minimal downtime.

When you’ll see results

Some improvement may be noticed within a few days, but results peak at about 2 weeks. A follow-up visit is scheduled to review your result and make small adjustments if needed.

How long it lasts

Results typically last about 3 months. Some people notice the effects reducing sooner, while others may last 4–5 months.

Downtime

Downtime is minimal. Mild redness, small bumps, or light bruising can happen temporarily, but most people return to normal activities the same day.

Aftercare

Avoid massaging or applying pressure to the face after treatment, stay upright for 4 hours post-treatment, and follow the aftercare instructions reviewed at your visit.

What to expect

Appointment steps

STEP 1

Assess + plan

We review your goals, assess your facial anatomy and movement, and create a personalized treatment plan for natural-looking results.

STEP 2

Prep

The treatment area is cleansed, and precise injection points are mapped to ensure accuracy and symmetry.

STEP 3

Treatment

A series of small injections are administered to targeted muscles to temporarily relax movement and soften the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Treatment is quick and typically well tolerated.

STEP 4

Aftercare

We provide simple aftercare instructions, including what to avoid and how to support optimal results in the hours and days following treatment.

FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does Botox last? +
Results typically last about 3–4 months, with some noticing it wearing off faster or slower than this. Over time, muscle movement gradually returns and the treatment can be repeated to maintain the result.
When will I see results? +
Some improvement may be noticed within a few days, but full results appear at about 2 weeks.
Is there downtime? +
Downtime is minimal. You may have mild redness, bruising, or small bumps at the injection sites for a short time, but most people return to normal activities the same day. Aftercare is discussed at each visit and usually involves avoiding massaging or applying pressure to the face for 48 hours, as well as staying upright for 4 hours post treatment.
Who should avoid Botox? +
Botox may not be recommended for people who:
  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have certain neuromuscular conditions
  • Have an allergy to botulinum toxin products or their ingredients
  • Have an active infection in the treatment area
Your provider will review your history before treatment.
What areas can Botox treat? +
Botox is commonly used for:
  • Forehead lines
  • Frown lines (between the eyebrows)
  • Crow’s feet
  • Brow lift
  • Lip flip
  • Downturned smile
  • Subtle neck lift
  • Chin dimpling
  • Jaw tension / clenching
  • Excess sweating in some areas
Your provider will recommend the best treatment for your goals.
How often do I need Botox? +
Most patients repeat treatment every 3–4 months to maintain results. Your provider can recommend a schedule based on your response.
Do I need a consultation first? +
A consultation is recommended to review your goals, discuss options, and make sure treatment is safe and appropriate for you.
