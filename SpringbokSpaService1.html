About

What this service does

Morpheus8 combines microneedling + radiofrequency energy to stimulate collagen and remodel skin for a firmer, smoother appearance.

Results you can expect

  • Improves texture + visible pores
  • Supports firmer-looking skin
  • Helps acne scarring + uneven tone
  • Targets deeper layers than standard microneedling

Ideal for

  • Fine lines + crepey texture
  • Large pores / rough texture
  • Mild skin laxity (face/neck)
  • Jawline / lower face support

How it works

Microneedles create controlled micro-channels while RF heat is delivered beneath the surface. This encourages collagen remodeling over time.

Details

Service details

Clear expectations — time, comfort, downtime, and what’s included.

Time

45–60 minutes (varies by area). Includes assessment + treatment.

Format

In-clinic appointment. Settings are tailored to your skin and goals.

Comfort

Topical numbing is common. Expect warmth + mild pressure during passes.

Downtime

Redness + mild swelling for 1–3 days. Makeup often after 24–48 hours.

Best results

Typically 2–3 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart (personalized at consult).

Aftercare

Gentle cleanse, barrier-support hydration, and diligent SPF for healing.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Quick answers. Ask us anything at your consult.

How many sessions do I need?
Most people do 2–3 sessions. We’ll tailor it based on your goals, your skin, and how you respond.
Is there downtime?
Expect redness and mild swelling. Many clients feel comfortable in 1–3 days.
When will I see results?
Some see early improvement within a few weeks, with continued collagen remodeling over 2–3 months.
Can I wear makeup after?
Often after 24–48 hours, depending on your skin and the depth used.
Who should avoid this treatment?
We’ll review safety at consult. Certain medical conditions, skin infections, or pregnancy may mean postponing or choosing another option.
