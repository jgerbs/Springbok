About

What Morpheus8 does

Morpheus8 combines microneedling + radiofrequency energy to stimulate collagen and remodel skin for a firmer, smoother appearance.

Results you can expect

  • Improves skin texture + visible pores
  • Firmer-looking skin and refined contours
  • Helps with acne scarring and uneven tone
  • Targets deeper layers than standard microneedling

Ideal for

  • Fine lines + crepey texture
  • Acne scarring / uneven texture
  • Large or visible pores
  • Mild skin laxity
  • Jawline / lower face contour support
  • Neck concerns (case-by-case)
Not sure? Book a consult — we’ll recommend the safest option for your skin and goals.

Service details

Clear expectations — time, steps, and what’s included.

Time

105 minutes. Includes assessment + treatment (and numbing as needed).

Format

In-clinic session. We tailor depth + intensity to your skin and comfort.

Comfort

Topical numbing is commonly used. Expect warmth and mild pressure.

Downtime

Redness and mild swelling for 1–3 days. Makeup often ok after 24–48 hours.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Quick answers. Ask us anything at your consult.

How many sessions do I need?
Most people do 2–3 sessions. We’ll tailor it based on goals and skin response.
Is there downtime?
Expect redness and mild swelling. Many clients feel comfortable in 1–3 days.
When will I see results?
Some see early improvement within weeks, with continued collagen remodeling over 2–3 months.
Can I wear makeup after?
Often after 24–48 hours, depending on your skin and the depth used.
