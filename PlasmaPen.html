What Plasma Pen treatment does

  • Softening of fine lines and improvement in skin texture
  • Gradual collagen and elastin renewal over time

Ideal for

  • Fine lines and early to moderate signs of aging
  • Mild to moderate hooding of the eyelids
  • Small, targeted areas needing lift or smoothing
  • Clients seeking a non-surgical, low-downtime option

How it works

Plasma IQ Pen is a non-surgical skin rejuvenation treatment that uses a focused plasma energy to target and treat fine lines, wrinkles, and skin imperfections. The device creates controlled micro-injuries on the skin’s surface, stimulating the body’s natural healing process and promoting collagen and elastin production.

This process tightens and lifts the skin, improves texture, and reduces the appearance of fine lines over time. Results develop gradually as the skin heals, revealing smoother, firmer, and more youthful-looking skin with minimal downtime.

FAQ

Questions, answered

When will I see results? +
Full results develop gradually as the skin heals and produces new collagen and elastin. Most patients see the final result of the treatment series around 3 months post-treatment.
How long do Plasma Pen results last? +
Results can last several years depending on your skin, age, and lifestyle. Because the treatment stimulates collagen, results develop naturally and fade gradually over time. Multiple or repeat treatments may be recommended based on your goals.
Does Plasma Pen hurt? +
A topical numbing cream is applied before treatment to help keep you comfortable.
What areas can be treated? +
Plasma Pen is commonly used for:
  • Upper and lower eyelids
  • Under-eye tightening
  • Smile lines
Your provider will recommend the best treatment for your goals.
Who should not get Plasma Pen? +
This treatment may not be suitable for people with:
  • Certain skin conditions, such as melasma
  • Active infections or wounds in the area
  • A history of poor healing or keloid scarring
A consultation is recommended to make sure the treatment is safe for you.
How many treatments will I need? +
Some patients achieve their desired result in one treatment, while others may need more than one session depending on the area and the amount of skin tightening needed.
