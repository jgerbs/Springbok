Dr. Kloppers prioritizes clear communication, careful assessment, and practical planning. His approach is grounded in evidence-based medicine, with an emphasis on prevention, chronic disease management, and continuity of care.

During appointments, the goal is simple: understand what’s going on, outline realistic options, and agree on a plan you can actually follow — with appropriate follow-up.

Treatment focus

  • Preventative care and long-term health planning
  • Chronic condition management (ongoing support)
  • Evidence-based diagnostics and referrals
  • Patient education and clear next steps
